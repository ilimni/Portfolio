About Me

The Person Behind the Work

✦ Accessibility First

I'm Jotham Kagah — an Inclusive Digital Experience Designer passionate about creating digital environments where everyone belongs, regardless of ability, background, or technical expertise.

My journey began in digital marketing and graphic design, where I discovered that beautiful visuals mean nothing if they exclude people. That realization sent me deep into the world of digital accessibility — learning, certifying, and applying WCAG standards to make the web genuinely usable for all.

Today, I work at the intersection of accessibility, design, AI, and communication — helping organizations build digital products that are not just compliant, but truly human-centered. I've remediated documents, designed inclusive learning platforms, built church media systems, and explored how AI can accelerate accessible content creation.
